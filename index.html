HOW YOU'RE SCORED

  • Words per Minute (WPM)

    • To calculate this metric, we take all the typed characters and divide them by 5.

  • ACCURACY

    • This metric determines how accurate all of your keystrokes are. To do this, we divide the number of correctly typed keys by the total number of characters and multiply by 100.
